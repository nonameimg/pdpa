PLACE OF PAST 2019

scent installation

Place of past project consists of the smells of 3 exfactories, which are now used for other purposes. I chose for example such places: Pluton, Winzavod and Bolshevik. The scent is presented in the form of a fragrance in a bottle, to create which I used both analogues of flavors for the base, and an additional essence, for example from nuts, which I produced at home. It is also presented in the form of a pack with objects that are either collected in this place, or somehow relate to what was previously produced there. With smell I’m trying to return a person «in the past» when these industrial facilities used for the purpose intended, because the sense of smell is one of the few feelings that can synesthetically affect human memory (the limbic system of the brain, which not only is involved in the regulation of olfactory processes, but is also responsible for emotions and memory), this process is called olfaction. Many industrial facilities are now used as art clusters, it gives them a second life, but such places have lost not only their function, but also a specific atmosphere. Place of past can be documentation, collectibles or an alternative way of capturing space. The phantom of the lost place is recreated in reality using a smell. Such packs or spirits can be taken on a walk in these places to feel what might have been there before. However, it can also be in the exhibition space with photos of Pluton, Winzavod or Bolshevik and a map where these places are marked
