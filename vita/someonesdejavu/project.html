A Someone’s déjà vu, Ostrava, 2023

printed RPG manual, RISO print, saddle stitch binding, 36 pages, 11 × 20 cm, 20 copies

This project is an RPG (Role Play Game) game that is based on dialogues between the players and the Game Master (GM). Each player creates a character and then together with the other characters they live the adventure that the Game Master has designed for them. By answering the Game Master's questions and making decisions together, the players explore the world in which their characters find themselves. Unlike classic RPGs with battles, spells and dragons, the plot of this project focuses on real-life situations.
