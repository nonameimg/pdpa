mirror infection, Moscow, 2018

interactive installation with EEG sensor

The project deals with the question of using the capabilities of the human brain and body modification. As well as the role of technology in our lives. Within the framework of the general concept of the exhibition (surrealistic fantasy on the theme of the clinic), a department for training telekinetic abilities was established. The project was implemented using a neural interface that is connected to the frontal and frontal parts of the brain. The device measures and displays the EEG power spectra. Sensors detect a subtle brain signal and interpret it into numerical values. So they show us our reactions to specific stimuli from outside. Visual images of the projection reflect these processes on the screen, thereby helping a person to understand how to behave in order to achieve the desired result, to orientate in time and consciously strain one or another area of the brain.
