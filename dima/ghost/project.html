the ghost of a non-place , Brno, 2021

game interface and newspaper

I don't remember how long I've been wandering around here. I'm a little confused about where I am. Until recently, I lived quietly here, where everything is clear and familiar to me. But at the same time, in parallel with this ordinary city, there was also its twilight ghostly part. The state of unconquerable strangeness that arose in this twilight zone permeated the home, and we ceased to recognize the very materiality of the place. Transitional spaces became centers of nervousness and at the same time peace. The blurring of the objects of the urban environment and, in the end, the boundaries of one's own body and subject led to the loss of a person's attachment to the sense of the individual. In ghostly twilight mode, the city lost its status as home when an unstable and alien disturbed its peace. And in this world of constant change, the memory of the place and other landmarks became impossible. There was a feeling of topophobia, which consists in the fact that a person really began to be in a free space, even if he was surrounded by known walls, since he no longer had the tools to describe them. Once the transition zones became the entrance to a new dimension, consisting of those very non-places that turned space into flesh, viscous and full of holes, where there is no island on which a person could build his "home". With the crisis of space comes the crisis of time and history, filling space with anachronisms. And in this timelessness, only nostalgia for the unfulfilled future, nostalgia for the lost organ of navigation, is stable. So I wrote this message in the hope of finding a place for dialogue that can restore lost connections and give a new, if barely flickering, landmark.
