traffic, Moscow, 2019

interactive sound installation

I would like to touch on the problem of choice and noise. This work is about capturing attention / space. About the problem of the pressure of invisible factors and circumstances that prevent us from concentrating on something really important. A collection of sounds that make up our daily background (notifications, ads...), will be displayed on the "partition" in the form of custom speakers. Each of them sounds special. Thus, this "kiosk" plunges us into a very uncertain state of a dispute at a meeting, a buzz in the market or just a situation where we have to choose 1 of the hundreds of types of cookies in the store. However, the viewer has the opportunity to resist these irritants. Physically using your own body overlays the sound of one of the speakers of your choice
